About Me

I love books!

My name is Tyler Gamett, I am a Junior in the Finance Program at Brigham Young University. However, this is not my only focus. I am also passionate about reading and sharing my thoughts on various books.

Reading and writing have been a side-hobby for most of my life. My imagination would always run rampant as I would dream of worlds beyond my own. The books on this website have been the catalyst of my creativity and imagination. Without them, my childhood would have been dull and uninspired.

My hope is that in an age of technology and decreasing attention spans, we can still find the time to appreciate the written word and the stories that shape our lives.
